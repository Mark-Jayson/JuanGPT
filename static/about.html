What is JuanGPT?

JuanGPT is a conversational AI assistant designed to provide intuitive access to demographic and social statistics data from the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA). By combining AI technology and user-friendly interfaces, JuanGPT empowers users to retrieve relevant data and gain actionable insights efficiently.

Mission

JuanGPT aims to bridge the gap between raw statistical data and user understanding by making PSA data more accessible and meaningful.

Vision

To empower individuals, researchers, and organizations with accurate and insightful information for decision-making and socio-economic development.

Why We Created JuanGPT?

The rise of the United Nations’ 2030 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) has inspired innovation through student-driven initiatives like hackathons and incubators. This increasing demand for accurate and accessible data about the Filipino people is essential for research, policy-making, and startup development.

The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) plays a crucial role in fulfilling this need by collecting and disseminating national data via its OpenStat platform. However, OpenStat’s interface can be difficult to navigate for those who are unfamiliar with it, and news outlets often present PSA insights without linking to the detailed datasets, limiting the ability for deeper analysis. This creates a gap in how users, particularly student researchers and other stakeholders, access reliable and comprehensive data.

JuanGPT was developed to bridge this gap by providing an easy-to-use, conversational interface for PSA's statistical data, ensuring that everyone—from students to professionals—can access and understand critical insights with ease.
