About JuanGPT

A conversational AI interface that makes Philippine Statistics Authority data accessible, searchable, and understandable — for researchers, students, and professionals.

Overview

What is JuanGPT?

JuanGPT is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) AI assistant designed to provide intuitive, conversational access to demographic and social statistics from the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA). By combining large language model capabilities with a curated vector database of official PSA tables, JuanGPT enables users to retrieve relevant data and gain actionable insights efficiently — with full source attribution.

Rationale

Why We Created JuanGPT

The rise of the United Nations' 2030 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) has inspired data-driven innovation through student initiatives, hackathons, and policy incubators across the Philippines. The demand for accurate, accessible data about the Filipino people is critical for research, policy-making, and startup development.

The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) fulfils this need through its OpenStat platform — yet OpenStat's interface can be difficult to navigate for those unfamiliar with statistical databases. News outlets frequently cite PSA figures without linking to the source datasets, limiting deeper independent analysis. This creates a structural gap in how users — particularly student researchers and civil society stakeholders — access reliable and comprehensive data.

JuanGPT was developed to close this gap by providing an easy-to-use, conversational interface over PSA's statistical datasets, ensuring that everyone — from students to policymakers — can access and understand critical socio-economic insights with ease, speed, and full source transparency.

Process

How JuanGPT Works

A three-step Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline ensures every answer is grounded in official PSA data with full traceability.

1

Submit a Query

Type a natural-language question — e.g., "What is the unemployment rate in Metro Manila?" — into the chat interface. No statistical syntax required.

2

Retrieval & Processing

The system performs semantic similarity search over the Qdrant vector database, retrieves the most relevant PSA dataset chunks, and passes them to the LLM as context.

3

Results & Citation

Receive a concise, readable summary with direct links to the original PSA source tables, ensuring transparency and enabling independent verification.

Data Scope & Sources

JuanGPT's knowledge base is sourced exclusively from official PSA publications available through OpenStat.

👥

Population & Demographics

Census data, population projections, household composition, housing characteristics, and geographic distributions from the 2020 Census of Population and Housing.

Labor & Employment

Labor force participation, employment rates, underemployment, occupational distributions, industry-level employment size, and labor turnover data from BLES surveys.

Wages & Income

🏥

Vital Statistics

Occupational Safety

Occupational injury and disease incidence rates, severity and frequency rates, workplace safety establishment data, and OSH policy coverage statistics.

🤝

Labor Relations

Collective bargaining agreements, unionization rates, strikes and lockouts, productivity incentive programs, and subcontracting arrangement data.
